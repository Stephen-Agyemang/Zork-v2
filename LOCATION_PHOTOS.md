# Location Photography

The game currently ships without campus photographs. The previous images were removed because their creators, source URLs, and reuse permissions could not be reliably established.

Future photographs should be added only when their provenance and usage rights are known. Prefer original photographs taken by the project author.

## Adding an original photograph

For every new image:

1. Take the photograph from a place where photography is permitted.
2. Avoid making identifiable people the focus unless they consent.
3. Keep the original camera file as evidence of authorship.
4. Record the filename, location, photographer, date, and license below.
5. Add the image-to-location mapping in `frontend/src/App.jsx` only after the record is complete.
